Privacy Policy – Planova

Last updated: 01.05.2026

Planova is an operational planning application designed to work without proprietary server infrastructure. The data generated by the app remains on the user’s device, except where required for the operation of the requested features.

1. Personal data

Planova does not require account creation and does not directly collect personally identifiable data such as name, email address, or contacts.

Any in-app purchases are handled exclusively by Apple App Store and Google Play Store under their respective terms.

2. Use of device location

Planova may request access to the device location.

The location is used exclusively to provide local weather forecasts within the app.

To obtain such forecasts, geographic coordinates may be transmitted to external weather services.

The location is not used for tracking, profiling, or advertising purposes.

3. Activity location data

Planned activities may be associated with a geographic location.

The coordinates used by the app derive from:

  • selection through map services
  • geocoding or reverse geocoding processes

This information is used exclusively to determine the operational context of activities and to process environmental conditions.

4. External services

For the operation of its features, Planova interacts directly with external services, including:

  • weather services, to obtain forecast data
  • geocoding and location services, to manage coordinates

Requests to these services may include geographic coordinates or information required to resolve locations.

5. Calendar access

Planova may request authorization to access the calendars available on the device.

If authorization is granted, the app may create and read events to represent planned activities.

Calendar management, synchronization, and sharing take place exclusively through the services chosen by the user and remain under the user’s responsibility.

6. Data storage

All application data, including activities and settings, is stored exclusively on the user’s device.

Planova does not use its own servers to store or process such data.

7. Data transfer

Planova does not sell or share personal data.

Data may be transmitted exclusively in the following cases:

  • requesting weather data
  • using geocoding services
  • accessing device calendars
  • exports initiated by the user

8. Third-party services

Planova may interface:

  • directly with weather and location services
  • indirectly with calendar services and payment systems (Apple / Google)

These services operate according to their own terms and privacy policies.

9. User control

The user may at any time:

  • disable location access through the device settings
  • manage calendar permissions through the operating system
  • delete app data by uninstalling the app

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions:

planova@bluewin.ch
